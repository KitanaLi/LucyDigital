Case Studies & Work Highlights

A collection of SEO, CRO, and Paid Social projects spanning multiple industries and platforms. Each case study reflects strategic thinking, collaboration, measurable results — and lessons learned.

SEO Strategy & Growth

Learna Group (Learna & Diploma MSc)

Complete SEO overhaul for eLearning group. Unified IA, keyword mapping, schema, GA4 tracking. +63.4% clicks (Diploma MSc) and +27.8% sign-ups in 6 months.

Learning: Entity-led IA + clean tracking accelerates compounding gains.

Togather (Event Marketplace)

SEO transformation across Listings, Supplier, and Venue pages. Keyword mapping, content differentiation, and Core Web Vitals optimisation. +51% clicks, +12% impressions, +94% /listings clicks in 6 months.

Learning: Removing duplicate PLP copy + CWV fixes = intent alignment + scale.

reBuy (Circular eCommerce)

Year-long visibility growth through competitor gap analysis, backlink optimisation, and content expansion. +22.45% clicks and +7.21% impressions YoY.

Learning: Balance technical SEO with content velocity in competitive SERPs.

Conversation Lab Portfolio (Various Clients)

SEO + Social for AHI Carrier, Environ, Inecto, Renew, Amakhosi Safari Lodge, and ASSA ABLOY. Consistent double/triple-digit growth: Up to +500% traffic (Renew), +85% organic (Inecto).

Learning: Localised intent + technical hygiene drives durable gains.

CRO & Experimentation

Inecto

CRO testing across landing pages and competition entries. +64% conversion rate via CTA and imagery A/B tests.

Learning: Benefit-first copy with simplified hierarchy lifts mobile CVR.

Environ Skin Care

Multi-market CRO for global campaigns. +8% conversion increase through form and CTA optimisation.

Learning: Align paid creative → landing page message to reduce friction.

Varsity College

UX improvement and form redesign project. +32% conversion uplift in under 5 months.

Learning: Shorter forms + progressive disclosure boost completion rate.

Luxury Travel Brand (Confidential)

A/B tested landing page experiences and form flows for high-value trip requests. Delivered a statistically significant increase in qualified submissions (under NDA).

Learning: Variant-level tracking clarifies match rate and API ROAS impact.

Paid Social & Lead Generation

Silverfin (B2B SaaS)

Multi-funnel LinkedIn campaigns driving MQLs and pipeline value. +67% MQL rate improvement YoY, €125K pipeline generated, ~53% ICP match rate.

Learning: Quality > quantity — doc ads + native forms yield better SQL value.

Each project reflects a data-led approach, balancing creativity, analytics, and execution. Where constraints existed, they informed better frameworks and sharper hypotheses for the next round.
