SEO

Search Strategy & Growth

Learna Ltd

Industry: Education (Online Medical)

Challenge: Niche medical programmes needing increased search visibility and qualified interest for course sign-ups.

Strategy & Execution: Unified IA; keyword mapping to high-intent queries; entity-led schema & internal linking; on-page optimisation (titles/meta/alt/H1-H2); mobile speed; sitemap & robots; GA/GTM/CRM tracking; briefs for copywriters; ongoing CTR analysis.

Results: +18.06% total clicks, +21.31% impressions (6 months). Overall sign-ups across Learna/Diploma increased +27.82%.

Learning: Entity-led IA + clean tracking compounds across funnels in niche education.
