SEO

Search Strategy and Growth

Learna Ltd

Industry: Education, Online Medical

Challenge: Increase search visibility and qualified interest for niche medical programmes. Grow brand awareness and drive course sign-ups among healthcare professionals.

Strategy and Execution: Unified information architecture across key journeys from awareness to enrolment, keyword mapping to high intent queries, entity led schema and internal linking, on page optimisation for titles, meta, alt, H1 and H2, mobile speed improvements, sitemap XML and robots.txt, GA, GTM and CRM tracking, content briefs for medical copywriters, and ongoing CTR and SERP feature reviews guided by progression rules.

Results: +18.06% total clicks, +21.31% impressions in 6 months. Combined Learna and Diploma sign-ups increased +27.82% in the same period.

Context: Group site with flexible online delivery and a broad MSc and PGDip catalogue.

Learning: Entity led IA and clean tracking compound across funnels in niche education.

Diploma MSc

Industry: Education, Online Medical

Challenge: Improve specialist programme discovery and conversion for postgraduate clinicians. Focus on programme level visibility, clarity of awarding university, and proof of outcomes. IA issues were not the blocker here.

Strategy and Execution: Programme level keyword clustering, enhancement of programme pages with clearer value propositions, structured data for Course and Organization, highlighting awarding universities, FAQs to match search intent, internal linking between profession pages and specialty programmes, and title/meta refinement to raise CTR. Included analytics and event tracking for enquiry points.

Results: +63.42% total clicks, +28.55% impressions in 6 months.

Context: Programme brand offering PGCert, PGDip, and MSc routes across specialties like Endocrinology.

Learning: Programme level entity coverage and clear UX paths raise discovery and enquiry intent.

Togather

Industry: Event Marketplace

Challenge: Duplicate PLP content, Core Web Vitals issues, and weak non-brand visibility across listings, suppliers, and venues.

Strategy and Execution: Keyword mapping, content differentiation by category, schema and internal linking, Core Web Vitals fixes for LCP and CLS, targeted content blocks, FAQs, Jira prioritised roadmap.

Results: +51% non-brand clicks, +12% non-brand impressions, +94% clicks to listings in 6 months.

Context: Two sided marketplace requiring intent specific templates for listings and suppliers.

Learning: Intent specific copy and Core Web Vitals hygiene scale marketplaces efficiently.

Social

Paid and Organic Social

Bloom Health

Industry: Health Insurance, Fintech

Challenge: Establish brand trust and drive qualified quote requests in a competitive, regulated South African market with limited digital visibility for new entrants.

Strategy and Execution: Integrated social, paid search, and on page optimisation with CRM tracking. Education led messaging on affordability and gap cover benefits, segmented targeting, high engagement creatives, retargeting, and automated lead routing for timely follow ups.

Results: Bronze Assegai Award for Social Media Platforms, Leader recognition for Search Marketing, SEO and PPC in 2020. Substantial uplift in qualified traffic, engagement, and quote conversions, with cost per lead efficiency outperforming industry norms.

Context: New entrant in a compliance heavy health insurance category operating under tight budgets.

Learning: Strategic storytelling with data driven optimisation builds brand credibility and performance.

CRO

Conversion Rate Optimisation

Inecto, CRO

Industry: Beauty, Haircare

Challenge: Lift landing page conversions.

Strategy and Execution: User testing, A/B tests on CTA placement, copy, imagery, and variant landing pages with GA and Hotjar tracking.

Results: +64% conversion rate in about 5 months.

Context: Focused on mobile journeys and competition mechanics.

Learning: Benefit first messaging and simplified hierarchy consistently outperform.
