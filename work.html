SEO

Search strategy and growth

Learna Ltd

Industry: Education, Online Medical

Challenge: Increase search visibility and qualified interest for niche medical programmes. Grow brand awareness and drive course sign-ups in a specialist audience of healthcare professionals.

Strategy and Execution: Unified site architecture across awareness-to-enrolment journeys, mapped high-intent keywords, applied entity-led schema, on-page optimisation (titles, meta, alt, H1/H2), mobile speed improvements, XML sitemap and robots.txt updates, plus GA, GTM, CRM tracking and ongoing CTR/SERP reviews.

Results: +18.06% total clicks, +21.31% impressions in 6 months. Combined Learna and Diploma sign-ups increased +27.82% in the same period.

Learning: Entity led IA and clean tracking compound across funnels in niche education.

Context: Learna operates the group site with flexible online delivery and a broad MSc and PGDip catalogue.

Diploma MSc

Industry: Education, Online Medical

Challenge: Improve specialist programme discovery and conversion for postgraduate clinicians. Focus on programme level visibility, clarity of awarding university, and proof of outcomes. IA issues were not the blocker here.

Strategy and Execution: Clustered keywords by programme, enhanced pages with clear value props, structured data for Course and Organization, improved internal linking and FAQs, refined meta/titles for higher CTR, added trust signals and event tracking for key enquiry points.

Results: +63.42% total clicks, +28.55% impressions in 6 months. Combined Learna and Diploma sign-ups increased +27.82% in the same period.

Learning: Programme level entity coverage and clear UX paths raise discovery and enquiry intent.

Context: Programme brand with university awarded PGCert, PGDip and MSc routes across specialties like Endocrinology.
