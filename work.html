Case Studies & Work Highlights

A collection of SEO, CRO, and Paid Social projects spanning multiple industries and platforms. Each case study reflects strategic thinking, collaboration, measurable results — and lessons learned.

SEO Strategy & Growth

Learna Group (Learna & Diploma MSc)

Complete SEO overhaul for eLearning group. Built unified IA, keyword mapping, schema, and GA4 tracking. +63.4% clicks (Diploma MSc) and +27.8% sign-ups in 6 months.

Togather (Event Marketplace)

SEO transformation across Listings, Supplier, and Venue pages. Implemented keyword mapping, content differentiation, and Core Web Vitals optimization. +51% clicks, +12% impressions, +94% engagement on listings over 6 months.

reBuy (Circular eCommerce)

Year-long visibility growth through competitor gap analysis, backlink optimization, and content expansion. +22.45% clicks and +7.21% impressions YoY. Learned to balance technical SEO with content velocity under heavy competition.

Conversation Lab Portfolio (Various Clients)

SEO + Social work for AHI Carrier, Environ, Inecto, Renew, Amakhosi Safari Lodge, and ASSA ABLOY. Consistent double and triple-digit organic growth rates. Up to +500% traffic increase (Renew), +85% organic (Inecto).

Paid Social & Lead Generation

Silverfin (B2B SaaS)

Multi-funnel LinkedIn campaigns driving MQLs and pipeline value. Achieved +67% MQL rate improvement YoY, €125K pipeline generated, and 53% ICP match rate. Key learning: quality over volume delivers better ROI.

Each project reflects a data-led approach, balancing creativity, analytics, and execution. While some campaigns faced constraints, every iteration contributed to growth insights and stronger frameworks for performance marketing.
